Who We Are

The Corporate Headshot Specialists

Northbridge Visual Group exists to make professional headshots easy for organisations. We come to your office, set up a professional studio environment, and photograph your entire team — efficiently, without disrupting your workday.

We work primarily with law firms, real estate brokerages, financial services companies, and colleges and universities. Whether you have 10 people or 200, we build a workflow that keeps things moving and keeps your team comfortable in front of the camera.

Every headshot is professionally edited and delivered within 48 hours. Consistent, polished, ready to use.
